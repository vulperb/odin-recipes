Spanakopita

Ingredients

  • Frozen spinach
  • Feta cheese
  • Phyllo dough
  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Thaw spinach
  2. Grill Onion
  3. Crumble cheese; mix cheese, onion, spinach, and pepper
  4. Mix oil, egg, milk
  5. Layer phyllo in pan with paste between
  6. After half phyllo used, layer cheese, onion, spinach, pepper mix
  7. Layer rest of phyllo and paste
  8. Cook at 375 degrees F for 1 hour
